RICARDO KAKA

Ricardo Kaka with his first and only Ballon D'or award in 2007.

  • Kaka Early Career

    Kaka began his football career with Brazilian club Sao Paulo when he was eight. His Sao Paulo debut came on 1st February 2001. He remained in the club for two years scoring 23 goals making 59 appearances with the club. He later moved to Italian club A.C MILAN in the year 2003 with the transfer fee of 8.5 million Euros and launched his career for the club Milan in the 2-0 victory against Ancona. While playing for the club Milan he won “FIFA World Player of the Year” in 2006-07 seasons. He stayed in the club for six-years bagging 70 out of 193 games.

  • Kaka Career in Real Madrid

    Kaka further signed to Spanish club Real Madrid for a six-year contract in June 2009. He launched his official career for the club on 7th August 2009 in the match against Toronto and scored his first goal for the club on 19 August 2009. Moreover, he left the club after playing for four successive seasons with scoring 23 goals showing skills in 85 games. Further, he returned to his previous club A.C Milan on 2nd September 2013. He was in the club for 1 year where he scored 7 goals in 30 games.

  • Kaka Career in Orlando City

    Kaka then switched to American club Orlando City on 12 June 2014. After his three Seasons of play for his club, he was sent to previous club Sao Paulo on 3 July 2014 where he bagged 3 goals in 19 gamed and returned back to club Orlando City. He is the good friend of Stefano Pinho and PC.

  • Kaka International Career

    This talented person has performed in various tournaments representing his country Brazil in both junior and senior levels. He launched his international career for his nation on 31st January 2002, in the friendly match against B0livia. He was one of the successful members of the team for winning the FIFA World Cup 2002. So far, he has scored 29 goals for his country.
